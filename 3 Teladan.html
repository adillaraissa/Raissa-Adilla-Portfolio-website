Being part of TELADAN

     After completing the first semester at university, I decided to take a significant step by applying for one of the prestigious scholarships, namely the TELADAN scholarship from the Tanoto Foundation. In early 2023, during my second semester, I was accepted as a TELADAN scholar. This opportunity is not only an achievement but also a profound pride for me.
      Achieving it required dedication, relentless effort, and unwavering prayers, for which I am grateful to have received this opportunity. In this program, I have actively participated in various self-development programs organized by TELADAN, one of which is the Lead Self program.
     This program has helped me to delve deeper into self-awareness, enabling me to pursue my dreams by maximizing my potential. This participation has significantly contributed to shaping my character and nurturing the potential I possess up to the present moment.

Journey at TSA USU

     In this journey, aside from participating in the self-development program LeadSelf, I also joined one of the platforms provided, namely the Tanoto Scholar Association (TSA). In the first year, I was part of the TSA USU 2023 management as a member of the project division and actively participated in various projects organized. Serving as the host for the event "TSA Goes to School: SMAN 15 Medan" was my first experience. Certainly, this role greatly helped me in developing public speaking skills.
     Additionally, some activities that aided me in developing public speaking skills include being the MC at the TSA USU Webinar: BATIK TSA USU 2023 (Casual Talk about TELADAN: Our Dream Scholarship 2023) and also being the Master of Ceremonies at TSA USU Pay it Forward 2023 WORKSHOP COURSED: Combating Sexual Abuse with Early Sex Education. Furthermore, I had the opportunity to be a facilitator for the event "Pay it Forward: Combating Sexual Abuse with Early Sex Education". I also actively participated as a volunteer facilitator focusing on literacy skills for basic education at SDS Yabes, which lasted for approximately 3 months. Then, in the TSA USU 2024 management, I was elected as the TSA chairperson

Another opportunity from TELADAN

     In addition to the various opportunities available in TSA USU, I also seized the chance to become an Assistant Guardian Angel (AsGA) in the Lead Self 2024 program. I am responsible for Tribe 1 - Djuanda Bijaksana, which consists of 17 TELADAN 2024 members, along with my AsGA partner and Guardian Angel (GA) seniors.
     This opportunity not only allows me to develop leadership within the academic and TSA scope but also enables me to assist others in their personal development. Furthermore, taking on this responsibility also provides me with the opportunity to develop myself in various aspects such as communication skills, note-taking, building relationships with others, teamwork, and fostering better responsibility and time management. By being part of TSA and various related activities, I have gained valuable opportunities to contribute to the community and also develop myself personally and professionally.
