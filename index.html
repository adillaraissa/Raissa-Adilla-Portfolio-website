I believe that through this education, I can broaden my horizons and skills necessary to achieve my career goals.
      Optimism and perfectionism are two traits that I carry with me on my journey of learning and self-development. Good relationships also help me to understand myself better and strengthen my leadership competencies. With a combination of a strong interest in leadership, an optimistic, perfectionist nature, and the importance of human relationships, I am confident that I can become a professional who can have an impact on those around me.
