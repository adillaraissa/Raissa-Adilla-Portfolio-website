Know more about me through my high school life

     As a science major student from SMAIT Al-Fityan Medan, my educational journey has been filled with exploration of my interest in the world of numbers. From the outset, my primary focus has been on the intriguing subject of mathematics, inviting me to delve deeper and comprehend its complexities. Intrigued by the challenges and possibilities it presents, I began actively participating in various mathematics Olympiads, although I have yet to achieve big awards in this regard.
     My involvement in mathematics Olympiads has not only provided me with the opportunity to develop cognitive skills but has also opened doors to a deeper understanding of the vast world of mathematics. Embracing these challenges, I have not only enhanced my understanding of this discipline but have also gained personal satisfaction from every achievement and problem-solving experience I encountered. Additionally, I am also interested in participating in writing competitions.

Active in organizations during high school

   Throughout my high school journey, I also discovered various opportunities that could hone my leadership skills. I actively participated in various school organizational activities that allowed me to sharpen my leadership skills. In the first year, I became a member of ROHIS with a focus on entrepreneurship, which broadened my understanding of the business world and creativity. Then, in the second year, I was entrusted to serve as the chairwoman of the OSIS akhwat, where I learned to manage teams and effectively plan various school activities. Additionally, I was also involved in various committees for school events, such as serving as the chairperson for the One Muharram event and as the 2nd secretary for the Al-Fityan Expo. Through these experiences, I not only strengthened my leadership skills but also developed a sense of responsibility and dedication to the organizations in my school.


Things I achieved in high school

     
     In High school, I consistently achieved impressive academic accomplishments by consistently ranking within the top 3 in my class and receiving several awards. By facing every challenge and opportunity with strong determination, I managed to maintain consistent academic achievements throughout my high school years. This success is not only limited to academic achievements but also reflected in being recognized as one of the top graduates during graduation.
