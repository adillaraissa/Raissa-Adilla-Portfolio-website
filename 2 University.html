Getting to know my university and major now

My love for numbers has never waned since high school, and it has even flourished as I delved deeper into this interest in college. As a student at the Faculty of Economics and Business, majoring in Accounting at the University of North Sumatra, I have been involved in various projects relevant to this field throughout my studies. From financial statement analysis to business strategy development, every lesson and project has enriched my understanding and skills in the world of accounting and education. It's not just a mere interest anymore, it has become a heartfelt calling to delve even deeper into the business world

Activities and projects

     Additionally, I have participated in projects focusing on processing plastic waste into economic goods, with a focus on SDG 12 at the Tempat Pembuangan Sementara (TPS) Garu II, Medan. Furthermore, I have been engaged in projects introducing and training the establishment of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (UMKM) in SMK at Sekolah Padamu Negeri Medan, as part of the implementation of SDG 8 carried out in the 1st semester. Through these projects, we aimed to empower SMK students with the knowledge and skills needed to establish and manage their own small businesses, thereby contributing to economic growth and sustainable development.
     I was involved in a project to create a company prototype in the strategic management course, which delved into company management and strategies in the second semester. This project lasted for one semester, providing me with a deep understanding of how the right management strategies can shape a company's long-term success.
     In the third semester, I also participated in a company project in the Management Information Systems (MIS) course, focusing on creating a prototype of a company management system. Meanwhile, in the fourth semester, I was involved in creating a company prototype by implementing the Accounting Information System (AIS), where my team and I had to develop a model that aligned with each prototype creation.
